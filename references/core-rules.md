# Core Rules

Use this reference for制度依据、文种选择、行文关系、公文要素、起草和审核底线.

## Reliability Order

1. Official rules and standards provided by the user or current official sources.
2. `党政机关公文处理工作条例` and `党政机关公文格式` related standards.
3. Official/authoritative media and professional writing guidance such as `秘书工作`, `党建网微平台`, `半月谈`.
4. Local转载 or training articles, used only as learning material.

If a specific policy basis matters and is not in the prompt, ask for it or browse/verify from official sources.

## Core Principles

公文处理 and drafting should follow these constraints:

- 实事求是: base claims on verifiable facts.
- 准确规范: use the right文种, authority, structure, terms, numbers, and citations.
- 精简高效: make requirements clear and avoid decorative verbosity.
- 安全保密: do not expose secrets, personal data, internal deliberations, or unauthorized decisions.

## Common Official Document Types

- 决议: meeting-approved major decisions.
- 决定: important decisions/deployments, awards/punishments, changes or revocations.
- 命令/令: administrative rules, major compulsory measures, appointments/awards where applicable.
- 公报: important decisions or major matters.
- 公告: important or statutory matters announced domestically/abroad.
- 通告: matters to be observed or known within a certain scope.
- 意见: views and handling methods for important issues.
- 通知: issue/transmit matters to be executed or known; forward documents.
- 通报: praise, criticism, convey important spirit, inform important situations.
- 报告: report work, reflect situations, answer inquiries from higher authorities.
- 请示: request instructions or approval from a higher authority.
- 批复: reply to requests from lower authorities.
- 议案: government submits matters to people's congress procedures.
- 函: consult, inquire, answer, request approval, or reply between non-subordinate organs.
- 纪要: record main meeting situation and agreed matters.

## Document Elements

Official公文 may involve: 份号、密级和保密期限、紧急程度、发文机关标志、发文字号、签发人、标题、主送机关、正文、附件说明、发文机关署名、成文日期、印章、附注、附件、抄送机关、印发机关和印发日期、页码.

Do not include elements unless the task requires them or the user supplies the values. Use placeholders rather than inventing.

## 行文 Rules

- 行文应确有必要、讲求实效、注重针对性和可操作性.
- Determine行文关系 by隶属关系 and职权范围.
- Generally avoid越级行文; if unavoidable, note need to抄送被越过机关.
- 上行文: usually use 请示 or 报告. 请示 should be一文一事 and should not be sent to multiple主送机关 unless rules require special handling.
- 下行文: usually use 通知、通报、批复、意见、决定.
- 平行文 or non-subordinate relation: usually use 函.
- Do not combine 请示 and 报告 functions in one document.

## 起草 Requirements

Before drafting, verify:

- The task is necessary and within the issuing body's authority.
- The policy basis is valid and cited accurately.
- Facts and data are true, current, and consistent.
- Measures are concrete: responsible unit, timing, method, standard, and accountability where needed.
- The structure matches the文种 and audience.

## 审核 Focus

Review drafts for:

- 是否确需发文 and whether content is consistent with laws, regulations, policy, and superior documents.
- 文种、格式、行文关系、主送/抄送是否合适.
- 事实、数据、名称、日期、数字、引用是否准确.
- Measures and requests是否明确可执行.
- 涉密、敏感、个人信息 and internal deliberation risks.
